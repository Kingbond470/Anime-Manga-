Anime (Japanese: アニメ) is hand-drawn and computer animation originating from Japan. Anime is a diverse medium with distinctive production methods that have adapted in response to emergent technologies. It combines graphic art, characterization, cinematography, and other forms of imaginative and individualistic techniques.
